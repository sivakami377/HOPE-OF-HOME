What we do

In 2004 on the outskirts of the famous city ‘Bhadrachalam’ on a plot of one acre, we built a Grace Aged Home for destitute and elderly people. On the first day, 21 people joined this home; currently, we are taking care of over 50 elderly men and women. When individuals come to Grace Home, we take care of all their needs.

How you can help

Our Grace Aged Home is purely run by generous contributions; we do not have any permanent resources for this project or financial support from the government. Please help us to continue to take care of these aged people. Please make a donation today and make a difference to lives of these aged people.
